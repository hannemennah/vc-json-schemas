Terminology

DateOfRequest

The date that the request was made.

DateOfEntryAndImportation

The date that the importation occurred.

ManufacturerSellerShipper

Identifier for the Manufacturer, Seller, or Shipper of the merchandise.

Carrier

Identifier for the Carrier of the imported merchandise.

EntryNumber

Entry Number assigned to imported merchandise.

InvoiceDescription

Description of the merchandise as included on the invoice.

InvoiceNumber

Invoice number corresponding to imported merchandise.

HTSUSItemNumber

Harmonized Tariff Schedule (United States) Item Number.

CountryOfOriginExportation"

The country from which the merchandise was originally exported.

CBPBrokerAndReferenceOrFileNumber

Customs Broker reference or file number corresponding to import.

To

Destination for imported merchandise.

From

Origin of imported merchandise.

Port

If the form information was previously supplied to U.S. Customs and Border Protection at other ports, the port of entry to which it was supplied.

DateInformationFurnished"

If the form information was previously supplied to U.S. Customs and Border Protection at other ports, the date which it was supplied.

RelationshipInformation

Relationship (if any) to the seller of the imported merchandise, including description and explanation of how this relationship affects the price paid or payable for the merchandise.

AdditionalCostsInformation

The date that the request was made.

ContractCopy

Copy of contract (or purchase order and seller's confirmation thereof) covering this transaction, and any revisions thereto.

MerchandiseDescription

Descriptive or illustrative literature or information explaining what the merchandise is, where and how it is used, and exactly how it operates.

SampleInformation

Breakdown of components, materials, or ingredients by weight and the actual cost of the components at the time of assembly into the finished article.

CBPOfficerMessage

U.S. Customs and Border Protection Officer message related to imported merchandise (if any).

ReplyMessage

Reply to U.S. Customs and Border Protection Officer message pertaining to imported merchandise (if any).

NameOfSigner

Name of Signer (Owner, Importer, or Corporate/Company Official)

TitleOfSigner

Title/Position of Signer (Owner, Importer, or Corporate/Company Official)

PhysicalSignature

Image of the physical certification signature on CF-28 form.

SignerTelephoneNumber

Telephone number of the certification signer on CF-28 form.

CertificationDate

The date that the certification was made.

CBPOfficial

U.S. Customs and Border Protection Official who authorized the certification of form.

CBPTeamDesignation

Team designation of U.S. Customs and Border Protection Official who authorized the certification of form.

CBPTelephoneNumber

Telephone number of U.S. Customs and Border Protection Official who authorized the certification of form.

CBPFaxNumber

Fax number of U.S. Customs and Border Protection Official who authorized the certification of form.

CBPEmail

Email address of U.S. Customs and Border Protection Official who authorized the certification of form.
