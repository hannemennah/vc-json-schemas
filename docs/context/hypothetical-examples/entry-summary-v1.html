Terminology

FilerCodeEntryNumber

Filer Code or Entry Number for Entry.

EntryType

Type of Entry.

SummaryDate

Date at which summary was made.

SuretyNumber

Surety Number corresponding to Entry.

BondType

Bond Type corresponding to Entry.

PortCode

Port Code that Entry was imported through.

EntryDate

Date at which Entry was made.

ImportingCarrier

Identifying information for carrier that imported the Entry.

ModeOfTransport

Mode of transport used to import Entry.

ImportDate

Date at which Entry was imported.

BLorAWBNumber

Bill of Lading or Air Waybill Number corresponding to Entry.

ManufacturerID

ID of manufacturer from which Entry was produced.

ExportingCountry

Country from which Entry was exported.

ExportDate

Date at which Entry was exported.

ITNumber

I.T. Number for Entry.

ITDate

I.T. Date for Entry.

MissingDocs

Information regarding any documentation for Entry that has been identified as missing.

ForeignPortOfLading

Identifying information regarding the Foreign Port of Lading corresponding to Entry.

USPortOfUnlading

Identifying information regarding the United States Port of Unlading corresponding to Entry.

LocationOfGoodsGONumber

Location of Goods or G.O. number corresponding to Entry.

ConsigneeNumber

Number for consignee corresponding to Entry.

ImporterNumber

Number corresponding to Entry importer.

ReferenceNumber

Reference number corresponding to Entry import.

UltimateConsignee

Name and address information for the ultimate consignee for the Entry.

ImporterOfRecord

Name and address information for the importer of record for the Entry.

MerchandiseEntries

List of merchandise entries, each identified by the following data:

  • HTSUSNumber

    • A Harmonized Tariff Schedule (HTS) number is a 10 digit code that identifies an import product to U.S. Customs and Border Protection.

  • ADCVDNumber

    • Antidumping and Countervailing Duties (AD/CVD) case number.

  • GrossWeight

    • Gross weight (in pounds) of Entry.

  • ManifestQuantity

    • Manifest quantity of Entry.

  • NetQuantityInHTSUSUnits

    • Net quantity in of Entry in Harmonized Tariff Schedule units.

  • EnteredValue

    • Value entered for entry (in dollars).

  • CHGS

    • CHGS information corresponding to summarized Entry.

  • Relationship

    • Relationship of merchandise to summarized entry.

  • HTSUSRate

    • Harmonized Tariff Schedule tax rate for merchandise.

  • ADCVDRate

    • Antidumping and Countervailing Duties (AD/CVD) rate for merchandise in Entry.

  • IRCRate

    • Internal Revenue Code (IRC) rate.

  • VisaNumber

    • Visa number (if applicable) for merchandise in Entry.

  • DutyAndIRTax

    • Duty and IR tax for merchandise in Entry (in dollars, with cents included).

TotalEnteredValue

Total cumulative value entered in Description of Merchandise section.

TotalOtherFees

Total of "Other Fees" values entered in Description of Merchandise section.

DeclarationInformation

Collection of infromation regarding the declaration on Entry Summary form, including the following values:

  • ownerIsAsShown

    • Boolean value representing whether the authorized agent is importer of record and that the actual owner is as shown on Entry Summary form.

  • ownerOrPurchaserOrAgent

    • Boolean value representing whether the authorized agent is the owner or purchaser or agent of the entity shown on Entry Summary form.

  • obtainedPursuantToPurchaseInvoiceAndPricesTrue

    • Boolean value representing whether the merchandise declared on the Entry Summary form was obtained pursuant to a purchase or agreement to purchase and that the prices set forth in the invoices are true.

  • obtainedNotPursuantToPurchaseInvoiceAndPricesTrue

    • Boolean value representing whether the merchandise declared on the Entry Summary form was not obtained pursuant to a purchase or agreement to purchase and that the prices set forth in the invoices are true to the best of the declarant's knowledge and belief

Declarant

Declarant information for Entry Summary, including the following information:

  • FirstName

    • First name of declarant.

  • LastName

    • Last name of declarant.

  • MiddleInitial

    • Declarant's middle initial (if any).

  • Title

    • Title of declarant.

  • PhysicalSignature

    • Image of the declarant's physical signature on Entry Summary form.

  • Date

    • Date that declarant signed form.

BrokerFilerInformation

Information regarding the Broker or Filer of the Entry Summary form, including:

  • FirstName

    • First name of Broker or Filer.

  • LastName

    • Last name of Broker or Filer.

  • MiddleInitial

    • Middle initial (if any) of Broker or Filer.

  • PhoneNumber

    • Telephone number of Broker or Filer.

BrokerImporterFileNumber

File number of Broker or Filer.

CBPInformation

Information on form that is for CBP use only.

  • LIQCode

    • LIQ code for CBP form processing.

  • ReasonCode

    • Reason code for CBP form processing.

  • AscertainedDuty

    • Ascertained duty amount for CBP form processing.

  • AscertainedTax

    • Ascertained tax amount for CBP form processing.

  • AscertainedOther

    • Ascertained other amount for CBP form processing.

  • AscertainedTotal

    • Ascertained total amount for CBP form processing.

  • TotalsDuty

    • Total duty amount for CBP form processing.

  • TotalsTax

    • Total tax amount for CBP form processing.

  • TotalsOther

    • Total "other" amount for CBP form processing.

  • TotalsTotal

    • Cumulative total amount for CBP form processing.
