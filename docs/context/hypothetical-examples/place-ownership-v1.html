Place/Land Ownership Vocabulary

A place ownership is an extension of the OwnershipInfo StructuredValue type to accommodate the ownership of a plot of land or geographical location.
  • [Latest JSON-LD Context]
  • [Data field origins: OwnershipInfo]
  • [Data field origins: Place]

Terminology

acquiredFrom

The organization or person from which the land was acquired.

ownedFrom

The date and time of obtaining the land.

ownedThrough

The date and time of giving up ownership on the land.

location

Location information corresponding to the [Place](https://schema.org/Place) which is owned.
