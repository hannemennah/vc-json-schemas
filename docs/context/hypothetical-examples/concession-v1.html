Terminology

administrativeArea

The area (in hectares) covered by the concession.

aggrementStatus

The status of the concession agreement.

allocationDate

The date or time at which the concession was allocated.

allocationStatus

The current status of the allocation for the concession.

concessionName

The name of the concession.

concessionType

The type of the concession (number).

contractor

The name of the relevant contractor (if any) who is or has leveraged the concession.

creationDate

The date at which the concession was originally created.

description

A description of the concession.

editingUser

The user (if any) who handled the most recent edit of the concession data.

finalConventionDate

The date at which the concession was originally created.

forestManagementCertification

Certification information regarding forest management.

forestManagementCertificationAgency

Information regarding the agency which has certified forest management.

forestManagementCertificationArea

The area (in hectares) covered by the forest management certification.

globalID

Global reference ID number for concession.

lastEditionDate

Date of the most recent concession edition.

lastEditor

Name of the last authorized editor of the concession data.

legalOriginCertificationAgency

Agency responsible for certification of the legal origin of the concession.

legalOriginCertificationDate

Date of the certification of the legal origin of the concession.

legalityCertification

Certification information for the legality determination of the concession.

managementCertificationDate

Date at which management certification was performed.

managementPlanApprovalDate

Date at which management certification was approved.

objectID

Local reference ID number for concession.

planningStatus

Status of the plans pertaining to concession.

provisionalConventionDate

Date at which provisional convention was provided.

traceabilityCertification

Certification information regarding traceability information for concession.

traceabilityCertificationDate

Date at which concession traceability certification was updated.

verificationOfOriginCertificationAgency

General information for the concession's origin certification agency.
